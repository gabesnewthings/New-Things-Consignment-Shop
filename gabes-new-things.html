Shop Policies

What’s going on here?
Here at Gabe’s New Things, we’ve asked artists to select one wacky little object from their life and put it up on consignment for charity. It’s as if musicians from around the world ran your local thrift shop and curated it with their unique junk.
‍
‍
Ok, I wanna buy something. Where does the money go?
‍For the first drop, all proceeds from donated objects go the Trevor Project, a national organization providing crisis intervention for millions of LGBTQ youth. Each drop will support a different charity.

Note: proceeds from t-shirt sales go to Gabe’s New Things ownership - Gabe needs to eat.
‍

‍
When can I expect my item to arrive?
Allow up to 5-7 days for handling time depending on your location. Please be patient, this is a new thing for us too!
‍
‍
I’m a musician and I wanna donate something! How do I do that?
‍Email us at newthings@gabegoodman.com
‍
‍
I bought a New Thing and something isn’t right! What do I do?
While all sales are final, if you have a serious issue (item never shows up, is totally different than what you expected), email us at newthings@gabegoodman.com
